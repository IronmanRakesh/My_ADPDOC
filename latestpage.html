DATA AT YOUR FINGERTIPS

Research-reviewed and scientifically-validated survey templates

As part of your HR suite, ADP Voice of the Employee makes it easier to not only set up and deploy surveys, but to help analyze your survey data as well. Take advantage of turnkey surveys and listen to what your employees have to say:

Engagement Pulse Survey - Understand how your team member are feeling at work. Leaders can use these insights to create the kind of culture that helps organization thrive.

Workplace Resilience Survey - Find out how your workforce handles challenging circumstances and events. It will help you find ways to support them when they need to bounce back and push through.

Employee Onboarding Survey - Learn how the new members of your team feel about their experience adjusting to their new job, work processes, and culture.

Exit Survey - Take a deeper look at your workplace from the experience of your exiting associates. Use the information to identify opportunities to improve retention and engagement.

Benefits Satisfaction Survey - The benefits survey helps you get feedback about the benefits offered to employees, measures the employee perception of satisfaction, understanding of their benefits, and how well they feel the organization is supporting their health and wellbeing.

Candidate Experience Survey - Use Candidate Experience Survey to improve your hiring process by understanding your new hires perception and satisfaction with their candidate experience.

Create Your Own Survey - Create a new survey from scratch. Add your own title, description, and questions with variety of response types for a custom designed survey.
